Journey

Before web development, my professional life had been a hodgepodge of side gigs. I wrote about Halloween costumes, waxed poetic about Hearthstone (no, really!), and even detoured to law school. Ask me about it sometime.

When I took a Javascript course in early 2021, I knew I had found my professional spark. It scratched my creative itch. It challenged me to solve real-world business problems. And best of all it let me learn, constantly, every day.

I have sinced plumbed the depths of Javascript, explored the beast that is React, and toyed with CSS frameworks while following The Odin Project curriculum. I built a ton of web apps and challenged myself to extend them with APIs, search features, and mobile-first responsive design.

If you need me I'll be off building something.
